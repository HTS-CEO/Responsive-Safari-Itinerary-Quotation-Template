Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi

Upon arrival at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, you'll be met by our representative and transferred to your hotel. After check-in, enjoy some leisure time to relax before your welcome dinner this evening.

Airport Transfer

Jomo Kenyatta International Airport to Nairobi Hotel

Our driver will meet you at arrivals with a sign bearing your name. The transfer takes approximately 30-45 minutes depending on traffic. Bottled water will be provided in the vehicle.

Accommodation

The Safari Hotel, Nairobi

A luxurious 5-star hotel located in the heart of Nairobi. Enjoy premium amenities including a spa, swimming pool, and fine dining restaurants. Your deluxe room features a king-size bed, en-suite bathroom, and panoramic city views.

Meals Included: Dinner

Day 2: Nairobi to Maasai Mara

After an early breakfast, transfer to Wilson Airport for your scheduled flight to the Maasai Mara. Upon arrival, enjoy a game drive en route to your luxury tented camp where you'll spend the next three nights.

Domestic Flight

Nairobi Wilson Airport to Maasai Mara

Scheduled flight departing at 10:00 AM with a flight duration of approximately 45 minutes. Small aircraft with maximum 12 passengers. Weight restrictions apply (15kg checked baggage + 5kg hand luggage).

Game Drive

Maasai Mara Game Drive

Your first game drive in the famous Maasai Mara National Reserve. Look out for the Big Five (lion, leopard, rhino, elephant, and buffalo) as well as countless other species. Your expert guide will share insights about the ecosystem and wildlife behavior.

Accommodation

Mara Luxury Tented Camp

Experience authentic safari living without compromising on comfort in our luxury tents. Each tent features en-suite bathrooms with hot showers, comfortable beds, and private verandas overlooking the Mara River. The camp offers excellent dining and a swimming pool to relax after game drives.

Meals Included: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Day 3: Full Day in Maasai Mara

Today is dedicated to exploring the vast plains of the Maasai Mara with morning and afternoon game drives. Optional activities include a visit to a Maasai village or a hot air balloon safari (at additional cost).

Game Drive

Morning Game Drive

Depart at dawn when animals are most active. Your guide will take you to prime locations for spotting predators on the hunt. Enjoy a bush breakfast at a scenic viewpoint before continuing your exploration of the reserve.

Optional Excursion

Maasai Village Visit

Experience the rich culture of the Maasai people with a visit to a traditional village. Learn about their customs, traditions, and way of life. Participate in traditional dances and see how they make fire using sticks. This excursion is available at an additional cost of $50 per person.
